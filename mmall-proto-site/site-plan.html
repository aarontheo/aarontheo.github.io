Purpose

Content

Home page

The home page is the landing page that greets customers when they come to the site, and shows off our latest products. The home page will have a large cycling hero image at the very top with a call to action 'SHOP NOW' button. below it will be smaller, more specific image links for highlighted products from certain categories, as well as the latest blog missionarymall blog post and some links to our social media.

Images for the Home page (more clothing images pending)

Stores/Contact

This page contains a list of physical locations, store hours, phone numbers to contact each location, and a general customer service number and email. The page layout has three columns, each with a picture of the storefront for each of the three locations. The images all have the address and a phone number for each store beneath them. Beneath all three, there is a number and email for our customer service.

Images for Stores/Contact (image for Sister MissionaryMall pending)

FAQs

A list of *FAQ*s *(Frequently Asked Questions)* about our company. These include questions about when and where the 2-year guarantee can apply, whether we are hiring, what systems of payment we accept, and so on. The page will be a list of these questions. When a question is clicked on, the section of the page answering the question unhides as a dropdown section, pushing the rest of the page down. When clicked again, the answer is toggled.
